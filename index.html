Brief Biography: Yuxuan Mo is a Ph.D student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Yu Hua. She obtained the B.E degree in Computer Science from HUST in 2021.

Her current research interests include learned indexes, persistent memory systems and data deduplication.

Publications
